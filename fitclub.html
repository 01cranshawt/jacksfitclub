About Jack

Jack is a fully-certified fitness instructor, with Level 2 and Level 3 qualification in personal training. After receiving his certification, Jack decided to convert a spacious room in his house into a studio gym. Using the money he had saved from a previous job, Jack created a space which contains all the equipment needed to achieve any type of fitness goal. Jack now has several private clients and runs a weekly boot camp, both indoor and outdoors. Jack is available to provide training in his personalised gym in Sheringham; home visits and outdoor classes across North Norfolk and Norwich.
